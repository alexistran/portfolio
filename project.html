Med Reminder

Aiding in the medication taking experience.

OVERVIEW

For individuals who have a medication regimen, one of the biggest challenges is being consistent with taking their medication, making sure that they remember to take it on time. Whether it’s because there’s too many medications to track, or simply forgetfulness, this is an issue because the medication is often crucial to the individual’s well-being. There needs to be a solution that can be seamlessly integrated into people's daily lives such that the problem of irregular medication taking is eliminated.
For the final project in my human centered design course, I worked to create a solution that would make remembering to take medication be an easier experience for people.

THE CHALLENGE

How might we help people stick to their medication regimen?

USER RESEARCH: IN-PERSON INTERVIEWS

I conducted user interviews with 12 people. These people include those who currently take some form of medication, or have in the past, and people who work in healthcare.

Interview guide for people who have taken medication:

  1. Do you already regularly take medication? If so, how do you currently keep track of when you have to take them?
  2. Where do you store your medication?
  3. What time do you usually take your medication and how often?
  4. What's your morning/afternoon/evening routine generally like?
  5. If you take both essential and less essential medicine (like vitamins), are you more prone to forget to take less essential medicine, compared to medication for more life threatening issues?
  6. What different methods in the past have you tried in the past to help you remember?

SECONDARY RESEARCH

I conducted some additional secondary research, exploring other attempts to solve this problem.​​​​​​​

Physical pillbox

    Pros

  • stores your medication
  • everything easily separated by the day you need to take it

    • Cons

  • does not distinguish what time you need to take your medication

Basic reminder app

    Pros

  • easy to use to get a simple reminder
  • already comes downloaded with phone

    • Cons

  • does not let you categorize your medication in an organized manner

Medisafe

    Pros

  • current most popular medicine reminder app in the app store
  • many useful functions

    • Cons

  • a lot of functions that might be overwhelming if your main goal is to simplify the medication taking experience
  • layout of pills is ambiguous, which can cause confusion

Through conducting secondary research, and seeing users use these products, I was able to validate a need for a solution to the original problem. While there are existing products that attempt to aid the medication taking experience, they either did not do enough, or had too much functionality that it was an overwhelming experience. My goal is to create a solution with a balance between both. ​​​​​​​
